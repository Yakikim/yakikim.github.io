### 砖驻转 砖 转拽爪转

````ad-code
title: 转转 砖 转拽爪转
```plsql
SELECT START_DATE
  FROM GL_PERIODS A
 WHERE A.PERIOD_SET_NAME = 'MLM_OU_Phiscal'
   AND PERIOD_YEAR =
       (SELECT PERIOD_YEAR
          FROM GL_PERIODS B
         WHERE B.PERIOD_SET_NAME = 'MLM_OU_Phiscal'
           AND TRUNC(SYSDATE) BETWEEN START_DATE AND END_DATE)
   AND A.PERIOD_NUM = 1;
```
````
